# Email Whitelist Guide for Roomy

If you're not receiving Roomy emails or they're going to spam/quarantine, follow these instructions for your email provider.

---

## Microsoft Outlook / Office 365 (Including University Emails)

### If Email Went to Quarantine (Microsoft Defender)
1. Go to https://security.microsoft.com/quarantine
2. Find the Roomy email and click **Release**
3. Check "Report this message as not junk" before releasing

### Add to Safe Senders
1. Open **Outlook** → **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Go to **Mail** → **Junk email**
3. Under "Safe senders and domains", click **+ Add**
4. Add: `security@roomylb.com` and `roomylb.com`
5. Click **Save**

### For IT Administrators
Add these to your organization's allow list:
- Sender domain: `roomylb.com`
- Sender email: `security@roomylb.com`
- SPF/DKIM: Already configured for `roomylb.com`

---

## Gmail

### Check Spam Folder
1. Open **Gmail** → **Spam** folder
2. Find Roomy email → Click **Not spam**

### Add to Contacts
1. Open a Roomy email
2. Hover over sender name → Click **Add to contacts**

### Create Filter (Recommended)
1. Open Gmail → **Settings** → **See all settings**
2. Go to **Filters and Blocked Addresses** → **Create new filter**
3. In "From" field, enter: `@roomylb.com`
4. Click **Create filter**
5. Check: **Never send it to Spam** and **Categorize as Primary**
6. Click **Create filter**

---

## Apple iCloud Mail

### Check Junk Folder
1. Open **Mail** app → **Junk** folder
2. Find Roomy email → Tap **Move to Inbox**
3. When prompted, tap **Not Junk**

### Add to VIP List
1. Open a Roomy email
2. Tap the sender's email address
3. Tap **Add to VIP**

### Add to Contacts
1. Open **Contacts** app
2. Tap **+** to add new contact
3. Add email: `security@roomylb.com`
4. Save

---

## Yahoo Mail

### Check Spam Folder
1. Open **Yahoo Mail** → **Spam** folder
2. Select Roomy email → Click **Not Spam**

### Add to Contacts
1. Open a Roomy email
2. Click sender's name → **Add to Contacts**

### Create Filter
1. Go to **Settings** → **More Settings** → **Filters**
2. Click **Add new filters**
3. Filter name: "Roomy"
4. Set: From contains `roomylb.com`
5. Then: Move to **Inbox**
6. Save

---

## ProtonMail

### Check Spam
1. Open **ProtonMail** → **Spam** folder
2. Select Roomy email → Click **Move to Inbox**
3. Click **Not spam**

### Add to Contacts
1. Open a Roomy email
2. Click sender address → **Add to contacts**

### Whitelist Domain
1. Go to **Settings** → **Filters**
2. Add filter: If sender matches `*@roomylb.com`, move to **Inbox**

---

## Zoho Mail

### Check Spam
1. Open **Zoho Mail** → **Spam** folder
2. Select email → Click **Not Spam**

### Add to Safe Senders
1. Go to **Settings** → **Anti-Spam**
2. Under "Email Address Whitelist", add: `security@roomylb.com`
3. Under "Domain Whitelist", add: `roomylb.com`
4. Save

---

## General Tips

1. **Check All Folders**: Look in Spam, Junk, Promotions, Updates, and Quarantine
2. **Reply to Email**: Replying to an email tells your provider it's legitimate
3. **Mark as Important**: Use the star or flag feature on Roomy emails
4. **Add to Contacts**: Adding the sender to contacts prevents future filtering
